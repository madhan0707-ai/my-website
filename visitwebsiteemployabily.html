Work experience while you study

Part-time jobs

Gain experience and earn money with a part-time job. If you have a Student visa, you can work full-time during holidays and limited hours during term time. Just follow your visa conditions.

Internships and placements

Many degrees include work experience. If yours doesn't, the university's Careers and Employability Service will help you find an internship or placement.

Student Ambassadors

Support the marketing team and represent the centre. Build your confidence, expand your network and make your CV stand out.

Virtual Internships

We've partnered with Virtual Internships – an award-winning platform for online work experience. They provide opportunities with 3,500+ companies in 70 countries. You'll complete your internship during the summer between your pathway and degree. Find out more when you arrive at the centre.

(Extra costs apply, 18+ only)

Launch your career in the UK

Want to work in the UK when you graduate? Good news – as an international student, you may be eligible to stay once you've successfully completed your degree.

You can find more information about post-study work options – including visa guidance, length of stay and how to apply – on Lancaster University website.
