Your Personal Tutor

From your first week, you'll be assigned a dedicated Personal Tutor to support you. They'll meet with you throughout the year, track your academic progress and be there to answer any questions.

Need help with an assignment? Looking for advice on the next step of your study abroad journey? Whatever's on your mind – big or small – your Personal Tutor will offer you guidance.
