Pre-Master's

Develop the academic, English language and study skills you need to successfully complete your Master’s degree at Lancaster University.

Why choose Pre-Master's?

  • Increase your knowledge of academic theory and subjects related to your degree.
  • Develop advanced study skills such as research, critical thinking and written communication.
  • A wide range of subject options, including artificial intelligence, business, data science, engineering, finance, law and management.
  • Regular start dates and 2–3 term study lengths to suit different English language levels.
