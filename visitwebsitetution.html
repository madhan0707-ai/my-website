Scholarships

Are you an ambitious international student? Do you have an offer to study an academic programme at Lancaster University International Study Centre? If so, you could be eligible to receive up to £3,000 towards your tuition fees.

And, once you start your Lancaster University degree, more scholarships will be offered.

  • For outstanding students
  • Up to £3,000 savings
  • One-off tuition fee discount

Which scholarships are available?

Regional Scholarship

Up to £3,000 one-off tuition fee reduction for your first year at Lancaster University International Study Centre.

To be considered, you must hold an offer to study an academic pathway programme at Lancaster University International Study Centre.

Scholarship availability and value may vary. If applying through an education counsellor, contact them for more information. For all other students, apply via the centre after accepting your offer.

Progression scholarships

Lancaster University offers a range of scholarships when you continue to your degree, which may include:

  • Global Progression Award (undergraduate): £3,000 per year of study.
  • Regional Progression Award (undergraduate): £3,000 per year of study. This scholarship award can be combined with the Global Progression Award, so students can receive up to £6,000 per year.

The Regional Progression Award is open to students from Bangladesh, Egypt, Ghana, Indonesia, Kenya, Malaysia, Nepal, Nigeria, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey and Vietnam who meet the progression requirements of the International Foundation programme.

  • Master's Excellence Scholarship (postgraduate): up to 30% off the programme fee depending on Faculty, as follows:
    • Management School: up to 20%
    • Science and Technology: up to 30%
    • Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences: 20%
    • Health and Medicine: 25%

Terms, conditions and eligibility criteria apply.
