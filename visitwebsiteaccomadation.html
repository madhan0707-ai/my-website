Your accommodation options

Lancaster University's unique collegiate system is at the heart of your student experience. Each of our nine colleges is more than just a place to live – it's a close-knit community where you'll find friends, support and a sense of belonging.

With their own social spaces, sports teams, traditions and accommodation, the colleges create smaller, welcoming communities that enrich your time at university. You'll find all the colleges located on campus, so everything you need – including the study centre, university facilities and your classes – is within walking distance.

Wherever you live, your college will become your university family – a support network that stays with you for life.

How do I book my accommodation?

Once you've confirmed your place to study with us, the Lancaster University Accommodation Team will get in touch to arrange your accommodation.

If you're under 18 on the start date of your programme: we'll offer you a room in our designated under 18s area on campus. For October start dates, you'll receive this offer via email in August. For January start dates, you'll receive it in December.

If you're over 18 on the start date of your programme: we'll ask you which room type you'd prefer. For October start dates, you'll receive an invite to select your preferences in June. For January start dates, you'll receive this in November.
